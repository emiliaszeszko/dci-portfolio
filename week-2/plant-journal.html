Keeping Plants Alive: 2018 Edition

This page was created to document the experiment that is plant care. On January 1, 2018, full of hope, I excitedly brought home over a dozen potted plants. I normally kill them with love (overwater), but this time will be different.

Plant Care Table

I wanted to compile all the knowledge gained from the internet into a handy table that would stop me from myself. Let's see if instantaneous access to information can help me and my not-so-green thumb.

Plant Name Water Sunlight Preferred Soil Toxic to Cats? Notes
Bamboo Palm
Chamaedorea seifrizii		 Water until soil evenly moist when surface feels dry; likes mist Bright indirect light; low light ok Potting soil Non-toxic Great air purifier!
Basil
Ocimum basilicum		 Keep soil moist Full sunlight is best; fluorescent lights ok Potting soil Non-toxic Prune before it flowers!
Christmas Cactus
Schlumbergera bridgesii		 Frequent and thorough, but allow to dry out between waterings; likes mist Bright indirect light; low light ok Looser soil (compost, loam, and sand) Non-toxic Propegates easily!
Echeveria
Echeveria		 Water the soil till it drains from bottom, then again when soil dries completely Full sunlight is best Cactus mixture (sand, topsoil, compost) Non-toxic Grows in a rosette!
Freckle Face
Hypoestes phyllostachya		 Keep soil moist; likes mist Bright indirect light Potting soil Non-toxic Also called the Polka Dot Plant!
Janet Craig
Dracaena fragrans		 Water when soil is mostly dry Low to medium light Loose potting soil Toxic Do not allow near pets!
Mint
Mentha		 Water until soil evenly moist when surface feels dry; likes mist Full sunlight is best Potting mix (sand, peat, perlite) Toxic Can grow in water!
Parsley
Petroselinum crispum		 Water until soil evenly moist when surface feels dry; likes mist Full sunlight is best; fluorescent lights ok Potting soil Toxic Turn the pot for even growth!
Primrose
Primula		 Keep soil moist; likes humidity Bright indirect light Potting soil Toxic This is a short term houseplant!
Rose
Rosa		 Keep soil moist; likes humidity Full sunlight Potting mix (compost, peat, perlite) Non-toxic Ideally grown outdoors!
